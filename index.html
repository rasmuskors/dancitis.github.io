What is Dancitis?

A physical state which causes the individual to experience the uncontrollable need to dance. Dancitis can strike anyone at any time regardless of dance experience or talent and is not limited to occasions when music is present.

Symptoms
  • Feeling happy to learn a new skill while having lots of fun
  • Feeling smart by learning the language of the body and memorizing movements which improve your mental sharpness, attention span and memory
  • You get better incommunication skills, passion, perseverance and creativity
  • You start building lasting memories and friendships
  • You get bursts of dopamine, serotonin, endorphines and oxytocin which are the positive neurochemicals that make you happy in every sense
